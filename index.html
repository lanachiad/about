About Me

Hiya! I'm excited you want to learn more about me!

I was born and raised in Charlotte, North Carolina, and moved to Raleigh, NC, when I went to college at North Carolina State University, where I received a B.A. in English with a concentration in technical writing.

My first job after college was as an SEO Copywriter for Three Ships, a digital marketing company that specialized in SEO. The company then split into three separate company, all with a separate purpose. The SEO-side turned into Demand Signals, which was then acquired by TRANZACT. It was after a year of working there that I found the opportunity to transition into the role of Junior Front-End Developer, where I've since pursued my passion for web development into Dev Bootcamp NYC.

In my free time, I love to read, play video games, and watch horror movies. I'm in love with that little pup in the picture, Django. I can recognize over 120 southeast North American birds by sight and/or sound. I'm also really good at sudoku.
